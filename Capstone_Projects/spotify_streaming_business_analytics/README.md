## Overview
This project analyses Spotify track-level data to identify key factors
influencing streaming success. The focus is on business analytics,
statistical reasoning, and managerial decision-making.

## Business Problem
Music platforms, record labels, and artists need to understand which
song attributes drive higher engagement and popularity in order to
optimise promotion and content strategy.

## Dataset
Spotify track-level dataset containing audio features such as:
- Danceability
- Energy
- Loudness
- Tempo
- Valence
- Popularity metrics

## Tools & Techniques
- R
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Correlation and statistical analysis
- Business interpretation of results

## Key Insights
- Certain audio features show stronger relationships with streaming success
- High energy and danceability are associated with higher popularity
- Some assumed drivers have weaker influence than expected

## Business Implications
- Artists can tailor production toward features linked with engagement
- Labels can allocate promotional budgets more effectively
- Platforms can enhance recommendation strategies
